About MN Bookkeeping Guy

Straightforward, dependable bookkeeping for Minnesota small-business owners who want clean books, clear reporting, and a real person they can call.

Who we are

We streamline your bookkeeping so you can focus on your business

Running a small business means wearing every hat — and the books are often the one that gets pushed to nights and weekends. That's where we come in. MN Bookkeeping Guy takes the day-to-day bookkeeping off your plate so you and your team can stay focused on what you do best: serving customers and growing the company.

Each month, our team handles the data entry, reconciliations, payables, receivables, and reporting behind the scenes. Then we hand you back the part that actually helps you run the business — the key financial details, metrics, and KPIs, explained in plain language. No jargon, no surprises, just accurate numbers you can trust and act on.

The result is simple: accurate books, clear answers, and less stress — and more of your time back to spend where it matters most.

  • You stay focused on running and growing your business
  • Your employees stop chasing bookkeeping tasks
  • We deliver the financial metrics and KPIs that matter
  • You get a local Minnesota partner who knows your business
  • Your books stay clean, reconciled, and tax-ready year-round

Our founder

Founded by Paul Mansur, CPA (Inactive)

Paul Mansur founded MN Bookkeeping Guy to give small-business owners the kind of reliable, approachable bookkeeping partner he knew they deserved. A Certified Public Accountant (inactive) and graduate of the University of Wisconsin–Eau Claire, Paul brings two decades of accounting experience to every client relationship.

His approach is practical and personal. Paul believes owners should never feel embarrassed about messy books or buried in accounting jargon — they should feel confident about their numbers and clear on what to do next.

Paul lives in Minnesota with his wife and their two children. When he isn't helping small-business owners grow their businesses profitably, you'll find him spending time with his family or out on the golf course.
